Serious Dog Club

It is a collection of 3333 randomly generated serious dogs with more than 120 different characteristics inspired by my dog. The goal of this collection is to be the best known collection of 2022, appearing in the spectacular of New York.

BENEFITS

STAKING

50% of the proceeds from the sale of the nft will be used in a Staking, to generate more value over time..

MARKETING

25% of the proceeds will be used for post-sale marketing which serves as a great value for the NFTs, the ultimate goal is to reach the screens in New York and deliver on the promise.

COMMUNITY

10% of the proceeds will be donated to the community, in money, merch, NFTs, etc. Rewarding the community and making it stronger and more united. It will also be part of an exclusive club that only NFT holders will be able to join.

OPERATING COSTS

The 15% will be used for expenses related to human talent, and to grow the collection.
